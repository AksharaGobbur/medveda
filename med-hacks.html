Med Hacks 2024!

Medical Innovation Hackathon and Healthcare Expo

An event by Med Veda Inc. in partnership with YAT

Are you a high schooler or middle schooler passionate about healthcare, and innovation, and eager to shape the future of medicine? Join us at our Innovative Healthcare Solutions Hackathon & Expo.

Date: January 27th 2024
Time: 1:00PM- 6:30PM
Venue: Heritage Park Community Center; 14301 Yale Ave &, Walnut Ave, Irvine, CA 92604
Registration Form

Event Logistics and Prizes

Project categories:
    Mental Health and Well-Being
    • Issues Addressed
      • Mental Health Monitoring: Focus on monitoring stress, anxiety, or mood fluctuations.
      • Real-time Support: Aim to provide real-time feedback or alerts for proactive mental health management. Give solutions on what they can do to calm down.
    • Objective:

      Participants have the freedom to propose and present innovative ideas for wearable devices (a drawing or digital simulation) or apps that monitor mental health indicators. There's no strict requirement for a working model; participants can showcase their concepts through presentations, app mock-ups, or design schematics.

    Telemedicine and Remote Monitoring
    • Issues Addressed
      • Remote Healthcare Accessibility: Explore solutions for remote patient consultations and monitoring.
      • Telehealth Improvements: Develop platforms for video conferencing, secure messaging, and telehealth appointments.
    • Objective:

      Participants are encouraged to propose innovative tools and platforms that improve access to remote healthcare. Presentation formats can include app wireframes or conceptual designs without the necessity for a functioning model.

    AI in Healthcare
    • Issues Addressed
      • Medical Imaging Analysis: Utilize AI algorithms for interpreting medical images, aiding in early disease detection.
      • Process Optimization: Implement AI to streamline healthcare procedures and enhance efficiency.
    • Objective:

      Participants are encouraged to propose AI-driven solutions to optimize healthcare processes, particularly in medical imaging analysis. Presentation formats can range from slideshow presentations to algorithm schematics or conceptual frameworks, prioritizing clarity and innovative approaches.

    Please note that all final explanations and end products, regardless of the chosen project category, must be presented through slideshow presentations, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the proposed ideas or solutions.
