Healthwise Tales

"Storytelling is the most powerful way to put ideas into the world today” - Robert McKee

Our Vision

Our vision is to transform the way children learn about health, making it engaging, accessible, and empowering. Through our innovative blend of storytelling and illustration, we aspire to instill in children a deep understanding of the importance of health and well-being from an early age. By fostering a love for learning and healthy living, we envision a world where every child has the knowledge and tools to lead a happy, healthy life.

What We Want to Do

  • Develop interactive books and presentations
  • Conduct virtual storytelling sessions
  • Organize storytelling sessions in pre-schools, libraries, homeless shelters
  • Collaborate with healthcare professionals
  • Expand outreach and awareness

Volunteers and Staff

  • Volunteers for illustrators
  • Story writing volunteers
  • Award PVSA to volunteers if they meet the requirements

Story Ideas

  • Nutrition and Healthy Eating: Teach children about the importance of eating nutritious foods and maintaining a balanced diet.
  • Physical Activity and Exercise: Encourage children to stay active and engage in regular physical exercise for overall health and well-being.
  • Mental Health and Emotional Well-being: Address the importance of managing emotions, coping with stress, and seeking support when needed for mental health.
  • Sleep Hygiene: Educate children about the significance of getting enough sleep and establishing bedtime routines for optimal health and cognitive function.
  • Hydration: Highlight the importance of drinking plenty of water and staying hydrated throughout the day.
  • Personal Safety: Teach children about basic safety measures to prevent accidents and injuries in different environments.
  • Disease Prevention and Hygiene Habits: Educate children about simple actions they can take to prevent the spread of germs and stay healthy.

Healthwise Tales is designed to be engaging and informative for children from a very young age, starting as early as preschool age. The stories are crafted to be relatable and understandable for young children, laying a foundation for lifelong healthy habits.

Healthwise Tales is committed to reaching children everywhere, regardless of their location. We host virtual storytelling sessions specifically for children, allowing kids from all over to join us! Our sessions are scheduled at various times to accommodate different time zones and availability, so you can easily find a session that works for you. Stay tuned to our website and social media channels for updates on upcoming sessions and how to join in on the fun!

Healthwise Tales is dedicated to making health education accessible to all children, including those from underprivileged backgrounds. We believe that every child deserves to learn about health and hygiene, which is why we conduct storytelling sessions in various settings, including schools, libraries, and homeless shelters. Our interactive storytelling approach makes learning engaging and enjoyable, ensuring that every child, regardless of their background, can benefit from our program.

You can stay updated with Healthwise Tales news and events by following us on Instagram. We regularly post about our upcoming stories, books, and storytelling sessions, so you'll always be in the loop with our latest updates and activities.
