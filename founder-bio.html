Founder Bio

Akshara Gobbur - Founder & CEO

Experience

UCI Health High School Volunteer

Volunteering at the UCI Douglas Hospital

Medical Innovation, Research and Entrepreneurship

I was recognized as the runner-up for my contributions to a groundbreaking initiative focused on reimagining traditional EEG technology to address unmet clinical needs in pediatrics. This program, designed to cultivate the next generation of STEM leaders, equipped me with invaluable multidisciplinary skills necessary to excel in the biotech entrepreneurial realm. During this transformative experience, I collaborated closely with leading professionals in innovation, medicine, science, and law within Orange County. Through project-based coursework, I honed my adaptability, resilience, and problem-solving abilities as I actively worked to tackle real-world challenges in pediatric healthcare.

Stanford Summer of Neurosciences Program 2023

As a participant in the Stanford Summer of Neurosciences Program 2023, I had the unique opportunity to engage with professionals representing a diverse array of disciplines within the field. This immersive experience facilitated invaluable insights into the paths to success of these experts, allowing me to broaden my understanding of the industry. The program encouraged students like myself to develop critical thinking skills by enabling us to craft our own inquiries and establish meaningful connections with established professionals, further enhancing our knowledge and capabilities.

Internal Medicine Shadowing, Dr. Mili Mehta

Completed an immersive virtual clinical education series to gain insights into the field of internal medicine.

Acquired valuable knowledge and experience in patient care and medical decision-making.

Associated Student Body - Treasurer (x2)

As ASB Treasurer in the Associated Student Body (ASB) and student government, I played a vital role in coordinating school-wide events aimed at promoting school spirit and unity. In close collaboration with the Activities Director, I managed the ASB budget throughout the year, overseeing fundraising efforts, supervising club fundraisers, and meticulously tracking financial transactions. I also organized a successful Multicultural Night event, showcasing my capacity to unite diverse communities through creative initiatives. Serving as a dedicated cabinet member, I provided oversight for numerous activities, demonstrating strong financial management, organizational, and event planning skills while fostering a strong sense of school spirit and community unity.

Student Coaching Middle School Science Olympiad Students:

For the past three years, I've dedicated myself to coaching middle school Science Olympiad teams, specializing in teaching complex topics such as anatomy and physiology. Guiding students through these intricate subjects has been immensely rewarding. Witnessing their achievements in winning medals brought me immense joy and inspired me to delve deeper into studying anatomy and physiology. The experience of teaching such complicated topics sparked my curiosity and motivated me to explore the intricacies of the human body further. Coaching has not only deepened my passion for these challenging subjects and pursuing a future in medicine.
